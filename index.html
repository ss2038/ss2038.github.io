My Story

My professional journey includes a rewarding tenure at LTIMINDTREE LTD in India as a Software Engineer, where I spearheaded the development and optimization of Chatbot functionalities using Python, React, and Flask, reflecting my meticulous attention to detail in enhancing code quality and security. Through personal projects, like leading the OptiDrive Development Team at UIUC and developing applications for real-time face mask detection and credit card fraud detection, I continually seek to apply my skills towards solving real-world problems. With certifications like Oracle Certified Professional in JAVA SE 11 under my belt, I am eager to contribute my expertise to innovative and challenging projects.
